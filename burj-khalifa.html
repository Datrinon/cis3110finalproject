At the Top, Burj Khalifa
2722 ft. tall ♦ 163 levels

A Pinnacle of Ambition: The World's Tallest Building

On presenting the original design for a 90-story Burj Khalifa to Dubai's Ruler, Sheikh Mohammad, Emaar Properties Founder and Chairman recalls the conversation he had:

“My meeting with him was very short. Really, very, very short, like ten-fifteen minutes and he just left. I had to chase him and ask: “So, should we start construction?” He said “I think you should try harder.”

Mohamed Alabbar, Emaar Properties Founder and Chairman

Indeed, it is awe-inspiring to see such an achievement realized by Emaar. Visitors to the Dubai Mall may access the Burj Khalifa from its direct connection to the Dubai Mall, nd touch the stars while immersing themselves in the region's young, yet rich, history.

Experience a breathtaking 360-degree view of Dubai skyline from three levels:

Level 124

Outdoor Terrace

  • Be thrilled by the world's fastest double-deck elevators, cruising at 10m/s.
  • Take a closer look at the world below through avant-garde, high powered, telescopes.
  • Step out onto the public outdoor observation terrace overlooking the ever-growing skyline.
Level 125

Arab Arts and Culture Exhibition

  • At 456 meters, Level 125 offers a spacious deck tastefully decorated in Arabic mashrabiya for stunning 360-degree views.
  • Set off on a virtual reality experience to the pinnacle of Burj Khalifa.
  • Enjoy a new immersive experience; step on an inspired glass-floor with a twist. Feel the glass crack underneath your feet, as you explore the lofty heights from 456 meters in the air.
Level 148

At the Top Observation Deck

  • Enjoy a personalized tour, guided by a Guest Ambassador.
  • Step out onto the world's highest observation deck with an outdoor terrace at 555 meters.
  • Refresh yourself with signature refreshments at SKY lounge.

Admission

Visits are based on 30-minute intervals with peak and off-peak ticket prices. We recommend booking your tickets in advance to avoid long queues and to make the most of the experience at your leisure.Immediate entry tickets are available in limited numbers daily.
